Interior

Designed to captivate the visitors' attention, the Interior is a physical interpretation of the brand our client wanted to achieve. The Pro elite salon is located in Mysore City in Karnataka with a royal heritage-style interior. It was a bare shell space given to us, which meant we had to design and construct every aspect, such as flooring, walls and ceiling. As a result, we created a subtle but bright interior space with accents of gray and wooden.

A conscious effort in choosing the color palette throughout the space helps accentuate and elevate the clients' mood.
