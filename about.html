Our Awesome Story

We work with the zeal to provide well-designed residential buildings for the clients within their budget, considering their needs and lifestyle. We understand that every family or individual is distinct, and adding flexibility to our work is crucial.

While some clients choose to use their team of builders or handle the interior design themselves, others want us to communicate their vision from start to finish. Regardless of scale or budget, we tackle every project in which we are involved with the same energy and attention to detail. ENNYESK Architecture has established an enviable track record with 'TRANSFORMING DREAM DESIGNS' to the clients. Our main area of competence is adding zest and originality to every architectural design.

Our Timeline

2021

Our company's tagline is "Transforming Dream Designs," which essentially means to turn your aspirations into reality and give your home the best possible appearance and experience. Over the past nine years, ENNYESK has significantly grown and delivered the best results to our clients.

This year, we were recognized for this achievement with SpaceiUX and Times Now awards. This turns out to be a pivotal moment for us as we gain credibility, notoriety, and the capacity to create more designs.

2016

The year 2016 marked a significant turning point for Ennyesk because it was one in which they reached major milestone. The residential project "chandaraangan" was a watershed moment in his career as an architect, which empowered Mr Karthik to move forward with future ventures and never look back.

By reaching such milestones, he could improve his work and understanding of his clients. In the future, Ennyesk aspires to be one of the top providers of architectural services.

2013

In 2013, Mr Karthik founded ENNYESK, quickly becoming the industry benchmark for the architectural sector. Mr Karthik was fascinated by the Indian infrastructure and wanted to build impressive architectural frameworks and win the public's trust to enhance living conditions in India and the surrounding areas.

His sole goal was to build luxurious structures no matter the client's budget, satisfy them, and provide them with the highest level of security.
